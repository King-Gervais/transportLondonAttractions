The National Gallery

The National Gallery is an art museum which was founded within 1824. The National Gallery is located within Trafalgar Square, which is within the City of Westminster. The gallery houses over 2,300 paintings, from the mid-13th century to 1900.

Primrose Hill

Favourite: favorite Comments: comment Share: share

Primrose Hill is a Grade II listed public park located north of Regent's Park in London, England, first opened to the public in 1842. ... It was named after the 64 metres (210 ft) natural hill in the centre of the park, the second highest natural point in the London Borough of Camden.
